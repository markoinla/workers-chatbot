Welcome to Ladders AI Chat

This page demonstrates the embedded chat widget with fixed sizing behavior.

What to expect:
  • A small blue chat bubble should appear in the bottom-right corner
  • Click it to expand the chat interface to full size (400x600px)
  • Click the X button to collapse it back to the small bubble
  • The iframe should now properly communicate with the chat widget inside

The chat widget uses AutoRAG for document search and OpenAI for response generation, providing high-quality answers about your project documentation.

Try asking questions about your project, documentation, or any topic related to your embedded knowledge base!
